Privacy Policy

Gwalava Boards and Furniture Fittings · Last updated 3 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gwalava Boards and Furniture Fittings ("we", "us", "Gwalava") handles information in connection with our social-media publishing tool ("the App"), which posts our own marketing content to our own social-media accounts.

What the App does

The App automatically publishes photos, videos and captions that we create to social-media accounts that we own and control, including Instagram and Facebook. It acts on our behalf as the account owner.

Information we access

To publish on our behalf, the App stores access tokens issued to us by each platform (Instagram, Facebook). These tokens are held as encrypted secrets and are used only to publish our own content. The App does not collect, store, or process the personal data of any other person, and it does not read followers' information, direct messages, or private data.

Information we do not collect

We do not gather personal information from visitors of this page or from audiences of our posts through the App. We do not sell or share any data with third parties. The App has no user accounts, analytics, cookies, or trackers.

Content we publish

The media and captions published by the App are our own business marketing materials (furniture boards, fittings and related products). Captions may include our contact number and website so that interested customers can reach us.

Data retention and security

Platform access tokens are stored as encrypted repository secrets and are rotated automatically. Media files are removed from our system after they are published.

Third-party platforms

Content published through the App is subject to the privacy policies and terms of the respective platforms (Instagram and Facebook by Meta). Please refer to those platforms' policies for how they handle content once published.
